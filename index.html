About Me

Welcome, I'm CJ! As a legally blind developer in training, my path to tech was driver by necessity, but has evolved into something more meaningful.

I began my studies in early 20251, following a path shaped by both personal healing and advocacy.

Today, I'm building accessible web projects that reflect my personal values and uplift the voices often absent from the tech world.

Why I Chose Tech

It started with one simple desire: to reclaim my voice and eliminate vulnerability after survivng unimaginable trauma. Survival was exhausting and I aspired to become the creator of my circumstance.

Coding has become a powerful tool for healing and advocacy. Through each line of HTML and CSS, I'm shaping a future not just for myself, but for others seeking hope and purpose.

My Mission

Turning Grief into Purpose

In the aftermath of profound loss (both the loss of my infant son to violence and the separation from my survivng children) I found myself facing incomprehensible grief but also the silence that surrounds mothers navigating complex trauma. I realized there were few, if any, spaces for us to gather, grieve, and grow through and with our pain.

Thats why I'm building more than a professional portfolio. I'm laying the foundation for a deeply personal project dedicated to supporting survivor mothers; those who have lost children under any circumstances for any reason and are searching for solidarity, visibility, and a safe place to honor their pain.

I aim to build a digital community where our stories can be told and our strength celebrated.

Creating BreakPo!nt, a Space for Grieving Survivor Mothers

BreakPo!nt represents the mission I carry forward to make sure no other mother walks the path of loss and survival alone.

This journey is more than my chosen career path, its my calling.
